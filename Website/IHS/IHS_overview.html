The Triage & Specialty Model

The FY2025 Operational Baseline reveals a system designed for two distinct functions. General shelters operate as high-volume triage centers to stabilize and route clients, while specialized programs deliver targeted interventions with high success rates. The operational data highlights a deliberate design where "Operational Visibility" extends far beyond federal compliance boundaries.

Operational Visibility vs. Compliance

The 56% gap between the Outreach App and HMIS is intentional operational design. The Outreach App captures the full spectrum of street encounters (979 individuals) to ensure operational accuracy and safety. The HMIS pipeline serves as the "Compliance Layer," capturing only those fully engaged and eligible for federal funding. This delta represents visibility, not invisibility, a rich dataset of pre-service engagement.

Triage vs. Specialty Outcomes

Lower exit rates in general shelters (Sumner, Kaaahi) reflect their function as Triage Centers. Guests are often transferred laterally to hospitals or specialty shelters, moves that don't count as "Positive Exits" in federal metrics but are critical stabilization steps. Once stabilized and moved to specialized tracks (VET, Family), success rates jump to 81-100%.

Utilization & Ramp-Up

Kumu Ola Hou and AOP opened mid-year 2025 and faced infrastructure delays. Kumu Ola Hou has since ramped to 92% occupancy. The primary question of "Latent Capacity" now rests with the VET House (29.5%). Is the eligibility criteria for veterans too strict relative to the population need?

Insight: Focus investigation on VET House eligibility criteria.

Shadowing Phase Objectives

1. Engagement Patterns

Investigate the "Federal Delta" to understand the characteristics distinguishing engaged vs. non-engaged encounters and their conversion patterns over time.

2. Specialty Handoffs

Map the handoff mechanics from Triage (General) to Specialty. Identify the specific success factors that make specialty programs perform so well.

3. Success Profiling

Trace data lineage for the 83% successful Re-Entry clients. Identify predictive markers to find similar candidates in the general population.
